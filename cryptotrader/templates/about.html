About Us

My name is Anthony MacKay. I wrote the Cryptocurrency Trader Assistant System as a tool to help me keep track of changes in the market and to help me with trading cryptocurrencies on an ongoing basis.

Right now, the website features a neural network system for future price prediction, a custom algorithm page where users can test trading algorithms they find online, share algorithms with others, or write new algorithms themselves, and finally a page where they can check in on the market's current price and recent price history and see what the graph looks like.

The site will soon be open to the public and anyone will be able to register an account and begin using these technologies to develop their crypto portfolio.
